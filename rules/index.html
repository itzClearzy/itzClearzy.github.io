Server Rules

All players are expected to follow these rules. Ignorance is not an excuse — read them before you play.

Last updated: May 2025. These rules apply across all game modes, Discord, and any Korvoxel platform. Staff have final say in all situations. If something isn't listed here but is clearly harmful to the community, it can still result in punishment.

🌐 General Conduct

  • G1 Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse of any kind is not tolerated. Permanent ban
  • G2 No hate speech — racial slurs, homophobic language, or discrimination of any kind. Permanent ban
  • G3 No spam in chat — repeated messages, excessive caps, or flooding. Mute
  • G4 No advertising other servers, Discord invites, or external links in chat. Temp ban
  • G5 No inappropriate usernames or skins. Staff may ask you to change them. Kick/ban

⚔️ Cheating & Exploits

  • C1 No hacked clients, cheats, or unfair mods — this includes kill aura, reach, no-knockback, and speed. Permanent ban
  • C2 No exploiting bugs or glitches for an advantage. Report them to staff instead. Temp ban
  • C3 No teaming in FFA modes unless a specific mode allows it. Warning/kick
  • C4 Allowed mods: Optifine, FPS boosters, minimaps (no entity radar), and cosmetic mods. When in doubt, ask a staff member. Info

💬 In-Game Behaviour

  • B1 No griefing outside of designated PvP areas or modes that allow it. Temp ban
  • B2 No impersonating staff members or pretending to have permissions you don't. Perm ban
  • B3 Do not argue with staff decisions in public chat. Use Discord to raise concerns professionally. Warning
